Mapa: as ermidas

Mapa com as ermidas e outros monumentos do culto cristiao referenciadas no subgénero literário denominado ˮCantigas de romariaˮ.

Map: hermitages

Map that depicts the hermitages and other Christian monuments that are mentioned in the minor genre known as ˮPilgrimage songs.ˮ

A toponimia das cantigas

Topónimos compreendidos entre a Galiza e o Norte de Portugal que som citados nas cantigas profanas.

Place names in the songs

Names of places mentioned in the lyrics. This map only depicts those place names located in Galicia and the North of Portugal.

Cartografia dos trovadores

Áreas geográficas e monumentos históricos relacionados com os trovadores (principal área de interesse: Galiza e Norte de Portugal).

Mapping the troubadours

Geographic areas and historical monuments related to the troubadours (main area of interest: Galicia and North of Portugal).
